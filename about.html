About

A lifestyle brand that promotes unity through different waves of culture.

Born in the nostalgic minds of friends and former teammates for the good old days and the longtime love for the game of basketball. Years of building muscle and memories, gaining skills and stories, winning trophies and tales. All woven into each and every piece of clothing that we set out to the world.

Our brand aims to embed sports, and the Cebuano/Filipino culture into our art in the form of our athleisure designs. We hope to invoke hope and inspiration for the young ballers out there and peace for the rest of the people despite the differences in culture and beliefs. We want to create a ripple effect and leave our mark in this lifetime. Here and across the whole world, within and outside of our homecourt.
