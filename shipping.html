Shipping and Returns

All orders will be shipped within 24-72 working hours. We do not ship on Saturdays and Sundays.

You can track your oder after we process it with our partner courier. A text message or email will be sent to you containing your tracking details. Please allow up to 24 hours for our team to send your confirmation message.

Orders will take 7 days or less business days upon payment of order. We currently ship via GogoExpress, JRS, and J&T.

We are currently offering international shipping via UPS. International shipping prices will vary on the distance the order will travel. For a quotation, you may email us at knowtheculture@yahoo.com or message us through our Facebook and Instagram pages.

We do not offer exchanges or returns. All sales are final. Our Facebook and Instagram pages are always open for more of your specific concerns regarding the sizing and detaied descriptions for each item.

Defective products can sometimes get past Quality Control. When this happens, you have 15 days from the day you received your product to reach out to us and have us look into the product in order to avail of an exchange for another product depending on the availability.
